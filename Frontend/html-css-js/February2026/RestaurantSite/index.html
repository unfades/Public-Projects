OUR STORY

Re-inventing Immersion Since 2016

For over 10 years, Leo's café has been bringing the spirit of gaming to our customers worldwide. Starting with a small corner shop in Brooklyn, New York, Leo's Gaming Café has expanded to 30 locations around the globe, including in major cities like Beijing, Singapore, Hong Kong, London, Mumbai, Seoul and many more...

Every experience at Leo's is bound to be remembered, whether it be a grand tournament for the newest Street Fighter, Mortal Kombat, or an unfeigned single player experience. The only thing we don't have is you. So come drop by, bring your friends, experience the latest and most trendy games only at Leo's Café.
